In this vlog episode, we join master boat-builder **Lameck Mukasa** deep in the lakeside forests. He guides us through the centuries-old ritual and engineering of carving a traditional *Byato* (canoe). 

From selecting the right *Muvule* tree based on leaf density to lighting controlled internal fires to hollow the trunk, this documentary captures the patience, precision, and spiritual relationship behind Bakenyi watercraft.

### Key Video Milestones:
1. **The Forest Blessing** (0:00 - 3:15): Asking the ancestors for safe tree felling.
2. **Rough Carving** (3:15 - 10:45): Shaping the bow and stern with hand axes.
3. **Fire Hollowing** (10:45 - 18:30): Using small embers to temper the wood and expand the seating hull.
4. **The Maiden Voyage** (18:30 - End): Launching into Lake Kyoga under traditional victory chants.
